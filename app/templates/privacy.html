What data we collect

{{ app_name }} is designed to work without user accounts. We collect only the minimum data necessary to provide the service:

  • Calendar data — calendar names, descriptions, event details, member names, and RSVP responses that you create.
  • Email address (optional) — only if you choose to provide one when creating a calendar, for link recovery purposes.
  • Session cookies — essential cookies to maintain your admin/manager session. These are not used for tracking.
  • Server logs — standard web server logs (IP address, browser type, pages visited) for security and debugging. These are automatically deleted after 30 days.

How we use your data

  • Calendar data is stored to provide the shared calendar service.
  • Email addresses are stored solely to send you calendar access links and for link recovery. We never send marketing emails.
  • Session cookies are used only to authenticate admin/manager access. They expire after the configured session lifetime.

Data sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal data with third parties. Calendar data shared via read-only links is accessible to anyone with the link — this is by design.

External services

  • Google Fonts — we load the Inter font from Google Fonts. Google may log your IP address according to their privacy policy.
  • Open-Meteo — if weather display is enabled, we fetch forecasts from Open-Meteo using coordinates you configure. No personal data is sent.

Cookies

We use only essential session cookies required for the application to function (admin/manager authentication). We do not use analytics cookies, advertising cookies, or any third-party tracking.

Your rights (GDPR)

Under the General Data Protection Regulation, you have the right to:

  • Access — request a copy of any personal data we hold about you.
  • Rectification — ask us to correct inaccurate data.
  • Erasure — ask us to delete your data (you can also delete your calendar via the admin dashboard).
  • Data portability — export your calendar via the iCal feed.

Data storage & security

Your data is stored on servers located in the European Union. Access to calendars is protected by unique, randomly generated tokens. We use HTTPS encryption for all connections.

Changes to this policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page.
